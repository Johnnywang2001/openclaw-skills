# Streamline Workflows with AI Agents

A practical, step-by-step guide for professionals who want to stop doing mechanical work by hand and start running smarter operations with AI agents.

## What This Guide Covers

- **How to audit your own work** to identify the highest-value automation opportunities
- **A four-factor prioritization framework** to choose the right first workflow (not too complex, not too trivial)
- **The three types of agents** — reactive, autonomous multi-step, and always-on monitoring — and when to use each
- **Step-by-step workflow mapping** before touching any tool
- **Real-world case studies** with hard numbers: Klarna, ServiceNow, Paycor/Gong, and a marketing agency that cut costs 96%
- **Which tools to actually use** — from Zapier for beginners to n8n for power users
- **The five most common mistakes** that cause AI workflow projects to fail
- **A concrete 30-day plan** to go from zero to one running automation

## Key Stats

- 57% of companies have AI agents in production (G2, 2025)
- Klarna's agent handles 2.3M conversations/month — equivalent to 700 full-time agents
- ServiceNow saves $5.5M/year from a single internal support workflow
- Median time to first meaningful ROI: 6 months

## Who This Is For

Business professionals, operations managers, team leads, founders, and anyone who spends too much time on mechanical work and wants to reclaim it. No coding required to get started.
